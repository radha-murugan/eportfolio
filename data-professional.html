The Data Professional

Learning Outcomes

  • Explore relevant industry practices, developing key skills for analyzing and transforming large datasets to address diverse business problems.
  • Gain proficiency in fundamental programming concepts and problem-solving techniques using Python and SQL.
  • Foster a culture of continuous learning in data science, enabling critical evaluation, design, and development of effective data science systems.
  • Understand stakeholdersâ€™ viewpoints, including data consolidation, preparation, and alignment with business objectives.
  • Learn to identify and mitigate cybersecurity risks in the data management process.
  • Critically assess emerging trends, professional standards, and ethical requirements in data science projects.

Collaborative Learning Discussion 2

Objective:

Evaluate an organization's IT Code of Conduct to identify:

  • Best practices, particularly concerning Master Data Management, to optimize data handling processes and ensure compliance with regulations.
  • Areas for improvement in the IT Code of Conduct to enhance clarity, accessibility, and effectiveness in data protection measures.
  • Incidents (anonymously) to assess the response protocols and your role as a computing professional in incident management and resolution.
  • Strategies for improvement, including updates to policies, enhanced employee training, and strengthening incident response procedures to prevent future occurrences and uphold data security standards.

The assignment was structured into 3 parts

  • An Initial Post
  • Peer Responses
  • A Summary Post
